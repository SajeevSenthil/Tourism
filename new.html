Visit Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu, located in southern India, boasts a rich cultural heritage and is home to ancient temples that showcase intricate architecture. Vibrant festivals, traditional music, and classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam are integral to its identity.

Visit Kerala

Kerala, situated on the southwestern coast of India, stretches for about 360 miles along the Malabar Coast. It varies in width from roughly 20 to 75 miles and is known for its lush greenery, backwaters and vibrant art forms like Kathakali and Mohini Attam.

Visit Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, located in the southeastern coastal region of India, is the seventh-largest state by area and the tenth-most populous. It is known for its major rivers like the Godavari and Krishna crossing its territory.

Visit Karnataka

Karnataka, situated in southwestern India, is one of the most prosperous states. It has made significant strides in education, industry, literature, and tourism. The capital city is Bengaluru.
